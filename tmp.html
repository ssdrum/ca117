Who are we?

We are five young, self driven students with a shared goal of helping to save our planet. Our aim is to encourage you to invest in not just our product but our planet, your childrens planet, your family's planet and your pets planet. Buy your Eco-Charger today and reduce carbon emissions, save money on electricity plus fuel and get active along the way!

What is the Eco Charger?

The Eco-charge is a eco- friendly phone charger. The charger works through kinetic energy created by the wheels of your bike as you cycle. This means by cycling you create a free renewable energy which in turn charges up your phone. The charger is compatible with any smartphone brand using a USB connector. There are also two charging ports meaning that it is possible to charge two devices at the same time. Our product along with its app gives cyclists the freedom to spend hours travelling, let that be to work, school or simply on a joy ride with no worries whatsoever of their phone running out of battery.

App Features

Charging on the Go

By simply peddling your bike, you get an unlimited supply of renewable energy for your phone. Eco-charge also stores energy that allows you to charge your phone while you are not cycling

Goal Setting

Create goals to improve your average speed, distance or calorie loss

Route Planner

Map out your route and get directions in real time. Easily find bike repair shops, bike rental shops, bike racks/shelters and charging docks

Lower Carbon Emissions

By cycling and charging your phone through kinetic energy, with our product you are saving the use of fossil fuels which are destroying our planet

Frequent Updates

Get notifications updating you on how much distance you have left to cycle till your phone is fully charged along with traffic updates

Weekly reports

View statistics on the amount of distance you’ve cycled, how much carbon emissions you have saved, your average speed, the total amount of kinetic energy plus phone battery generated and some tips on how to improve
